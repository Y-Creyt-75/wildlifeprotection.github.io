Trade Suspensions Must Be Maintained on Non-Compliant Nations.

By Environmental Investigation Agency (EIA).| October 15, 2024

The CITES Standing Committee (SC79) is focusing on enforcement and compliance matters, particularly regarding Laos. The country is currently under trade suspensions due to persistent and open illegal trade in tiger, rhino, bear, elephant, and pangolin parts operating through retail units. EIA is urging CITES parties to maintain these suspensions until Laos provides concrete evidence of effective action against the wildlife crime syndicates.

Urgent Action Needed to Save the Near-Extinct Vaquita Porpoise

By Environmental Investigation Agency (EIA).| October 15, 2024

The Vaquita Porpoise, listed among the world's most threatened species, is a critical area of focus. CITES is being urged to enhance measures to protect the Vaquita from extinction, which is primarily driven by illegal fishing practices that use gillnets in its habitat. The meeting will stress the need for strengthened international cooperation to enforce a zero-tolerance approach to these destructive fishing methods.
